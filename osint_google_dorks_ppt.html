Investigation Limitations

Private Profiles

Profiles protected behind platform privacy barriers are inaccessible and excluded from public search indexing engines.

Indexing Latency

Google search results depend entirely on crawl schedules. Recent updates or changes can take weeks to index.

False Positives

Identical handles can easily belong to separate users across platforms, raising high false correlation risks.

Ethical & Legal Guidelines

Operational Parameters

  • Public Data Only: Rely strictly on open source, publicly indexed data.
  • No Unauthorized Access: Never attempt login hacks or system circumvention.
  • Respect Privacy Laws: Adhere strictly to regional and platform policy frameworks.

Standard Compliance

Integrity-first operational parameters protect investigators from legal liabilities while maintaining standard chain-of-custody compliance during open source collection.
